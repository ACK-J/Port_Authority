Allowlist

Allowed domains, IP addresses, and CIDR ranges can make local requests and load LexisNexis tracking scripts without being blocked. Domains match the page making the request. IP and CIDR entries also allow connections to those addresses.

Examples: discord.com, 127.0.0.1, 192.168.1.0/24.

Cross-Origin Local Access

Saved "Always Allow" permissions for internet pages that may navigate to a local address. Each entry is an origin → local destination pair.
